We’ve tried to keep it as simple as possible, but if you’re not familiar with terms like cookies, IP addresses, pixel tags and browsers, then read about these key terms first. Your privacy matters to noteworld so whether you are new to noteworld or a longtime user, please do take the time to get to know our practices.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users. Please note that there is not information that will be permanently stored with us. We also do not share any of your information with any third party.

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information we get from your use of our service. We collect information about the services that you use and how you use it. This information includes:

    • Location Information

      When you use noteworld services, we may collect and process information about your actual location. We use various technologies to determine location, including IP address, GPS, and other sensors that may, for example, provide us with information on nearby devices, Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.

    • Log data

      Whenever you use noteworld, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing my Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

    • Local storage

      Your data is primarly stored inside of your own device local storage from which you use to access the website.

  • Information you give us. For example, our service requires you to send us locations you'd like to remember or note. Those informations are cruical that we store them for you safely incase anything happens on your end.
