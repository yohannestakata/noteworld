User guide

1

First look

Noteworld's interface will look like the following image when first loaded. It contains a side navigation with four buttons, a map and an area where you will find your pinned notes.

The home button on your navigation bar takes you back to the homepage. The note icon should reveal and hide your notes helping you have more space to use the map. The sun icon should help you in choosing different map layers. The last pallette icon will change the theme of the interface. You can customize the interface with any theme/ map layer combination you'd like.

2

Finding your location

The map will be displayed in your general location by default. You can zoom in or out to find a location you'd like to pin. Once you find your location it's time to take notes.

3

How to take notes.

Clicking anywhere on the map will diplay a form. The form contains a two fields; one for your titles and another for your notes. Once you have filled them out hitting submit will save your note.

4

Where to find your notes

Once you have submitted your notes, they will automatically be transported to the notes section. There you can find every note you have taken. Clicking on the note will navigate the map to the location the note was taken at. The map might sometimes take a while to render depending on your location and connection speed.
